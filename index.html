About Us

Samhitha Educational Institution, approved by Adikavi Nannayya University is a 15-year-old premier organization specialized in UG courses and has been being consistently ranked among the top Degree colleges in coastal districts. In a parent survey conducted recently, Samhitha was considered one of the best educational institutes in this region, with a proven record of achievements and especially with its own spacious infrastructure. In the historical city of Rajahmundry & on the serene bank of the pious Godavari River in the heart of the city, at Taditota Junction, Samhitha is situated proudly serving vast student community.

OUR VISION

To be an institution of excellence for holistic development creating a supportive, creative & productive learning environment which is accessible, affordable & innovative and to instill prime human values and thoughtfulness in our students.

OUR MISSION

We are on a mission to impart value-based education to all students coming from various social, economical and cultural backgrounds.
